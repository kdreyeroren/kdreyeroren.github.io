Travel Information

Basics

All wedding events will be held at East Hill Farm in Troy, NH.

Flights

The closest major airport is Logan Airport in Boston (BOS), about an hour and 45 minutes away. There is also a small airport in Manchester, NH, which is about an hour and 15 minutes away.

Lodging

You'll be able to book a room at the Inn at East Hill Farm for Sunday night (9/24/17) and, if you'll be attending the rehearsal dinner, Saturday night (9/23/2017) as well. Please call the Inn's reservations line at 1-800-242-6495 to book your room. Feel free to stay at any of the rooms, either in the main inn or in the numerous other lodging options on the premises. The only one I don't recommend is the Brick House, because it is a few minutes' drive from the farm and it'll be more pleasant for everyone if you can just waltz back to your bedroom at the end of the evening.
